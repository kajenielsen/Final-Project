How to play Wordle

Wordle is a word puzzle game where the player tries to guess a secret word. Here's how to play:

  1. Click on a box, then start typing with the digital keyboard.
  2. Begin on the first row, then click "Guess" to make a guess.
  3. The letters will change color to indicate correctness:
    • Green: Correct and in the correct position.
    • Yellow: Correct but in the wrong position.
    • Red: Word does not contain letter.
  4. Keep guessing until you find the correct word or run out of attempts!
